Website Evaluations

Hyer

Hyer's website looks clean. its easy to follow along and see all they have to offer

  • Contrast: Excellent contrast between text and background for readability.
  • Repetition: same font and color scheme throughout.
  • Alignment: elements are alligned alternating left and right sides between sections so its easy to tell youre in a new topic area.
  • Proximity: they did a good job sectioning things together that should be and keeping it simple

Overall, Hyer follows the CRAP principles and have created a user-friendly experience that is visually appealing and easy to navigate.

blinkee

blinkee's website has awful contrast, some of the words are blurry, and no links has the hover effect making it difficult to use.

  • Contrast: dummy contrast makes text hard to read against the wild background.
  • Repetition: their colors are all over the place and it makes the page look chaotic.
  • Alignment: everything can move with the mouse. they basically have two footers on the page in different areas.
